favorite one?

HAHAHAHA just kidding >.<, what if you really got none of these mentioned just now? Okay, the last thing you need is a pretty face or some talents.


Guess you already know why

The changes of the times have changed many things, but there is one thing that will never change. That is, people like beautiful things. Beautiful people will be more valued. Although this is not a good phenomenon, it is a fact. Throughout each dynasty, there are many childless and weak family background women who rely on their beauty or talent to win the emperor's favor.

Even you are pretty enough
but you still cannot lack of these

You need to be virgin

Yes, if you want to be a concubine, one of the requirement is you have to still be a virgin before you enter the palace. Also, only ages between 13-16 are allowed to participate in the draft.

You need to have a clean armpits.

This one might sounds weird but it is real!!! There will be some specific Laomomo (老嬷嬷) to test this during the draft. Your armpits cannot be smelly and you need to make sure that you don't have body odour. Or...i mean if you have it then the emperor gon kill you for sure...

Dignified etiquette

Have you ever see a concubine fart everywhere as she wanted? Of course no! To be a concubine, you need to have a dignified etiquette. Steps should be small when walking, no sound when eating, and no hiccups at will. Life is so restrict as a concubine right? But if you want the honour, you have to pay the price

Know more about the harem

Beauty is important, but when you go back and look at the pictures of ancient concubines, you will find that they are actually pretty ordinary. This is because ancient marriages were mostly political marriages, just for the country to get along with other tribes, or for ministers to continue to be loyal to the country. The emperor could not give too much love to the beautiful concubine but with weak family background, because her family could not do much to help the emperor's affairs. If you give too much pampering, you will be regarded as a bane of the country.
