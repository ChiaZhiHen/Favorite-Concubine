How to be the emperor's
favorite one?

First, you need to have a very strong and powerful family background which is able to help the emperor in the imperial court.


Why is family background important?

The political field is closely related to the harem, and everything the concubine’s family does will affect her status in the harem. For example, if you have a father who is a minister, then your position in the harem is naturally higher. Conversely, if the concubine's family is in straitened circumstances, without a strong family background, your status will naturally be low. Yeah, the world is realistic, if you got a rich and powerful father then your life in the harem is already guaranteed. So get yourself a rich sugardaddy (Just kidding LMAO, let's be serious >.-)!

Know more about the harem

Empress is the wife of emperor (正妻), and all the other women in the harem can only be called as concubine (妾). If the first wife pass away, the emperor usually will confer the title of empress to another high position concubine with strong family background in the harem so there will be someone who can helps emperor to take control of the harem, and they will be call as second empress (继后). However, there will still be some cases where the concubine get the title of empress after she passed away, and it is called "posthumous enfeoffment（追封）". It usually will only happen when the concubine's son becomes the next emperor after she died.
