How to be the emperor's
favorite one?

So you tell ME! that you don't have a strong family background? But you still wanna be the emperor's favorite one? Well it's okay, let me tell you the second factor to be loved. It is to have kids.


To have kids? Why?

Before you ask why, think about it first. Where does all the next emperor come from? Is it from the street? Definitely no LMAO. Is it god send from the heaven? Well...maybe but not really. Is it from the concubine of the emperor? Yes! This is why you should have kids, it will improve your position in the harem. More kids= more love= higher position. As they always say, the mother's honour increases as her son's position rises(母凭子贵). If you are lucky enough, your son will be the next emperor and you will be the queen mother, the woman that hold the highest power.

Some concubine with weak family background
but reached a high position because of kids

Empress Xiaoyichun (孝仪纯皇后), Surname: Weijia (魏佳氏)

The third wife (posthumous enfeoffment) of QianLong Emperor.
Her originally family background: Baoyi (包衣), a group of servants in the homes of the emperor and clan princes.
Peak position in the harem: Empress (皇后), first rank in the harem (posthumous enfeoffment)
Amount of kid (s): 6 (4 princes, 2 princesess)
She never experience the life as a Queen Mother even her son Yongyan (顒琰) enthrone as Jiaqing Emperor (嘉庆帝) because she already passed away when her son enthrones.

Consort Liang (良妃), Surname: Wei (卫氏)

A beloved concubine of Kangxi Emperor (康熙帝). Her orgianally family background: Baoyi (包衣), a group of servants in the homes of the emperor and clan princes. Peak position in the harem: Consort (妃), fourth rank in the harem. Amount of kid (s): 1 prince, Yunsi (允禩), the eighth son of Kangxi Emperor

Empress Xiaogongren (孝恭仁皇后), Surname: Wuya (乌雅氏)

The queen mother of Yongzheng Emperor (雍正帝). Her originally family background: Baoyi (包衣), a group of servants in the homes of the emperor and clan princes. Peak position in the harem: Consort (妃), fourth rank in the harem. She will lastly be crowned as the queen mother after her son Yinzhen (胤禛) enthrone as Yongzheng Emperor. Amount of kid (s): 6 (3 princes, 3 princesess)

Know more about the harem

In ancient times, family background was very important. Generally, girls with a bad family background would not have the opportunity to enter the palace, let alone become the emperor’s concubine. But the emperor may not like a woman of good family background. Then some palace maids (宫女) who were drafted into the palace from the House of Internal Affairs (内务府) have the opportunity to become the emperor's new favorite. Once liked by the emperor, she can call herself the "young mistress (小主)" after the canonization. As long as you give birth to a child, you will have the opportunity to raise the flag (抬旗，简单来说就是把自己全家人的身份抬高) for the whole family and improve your family background.
